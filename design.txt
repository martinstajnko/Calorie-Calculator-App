Title: Calorie Calculator

Description: An app that gets from the user the user's weight, height,
age, gender, city and country, and scrapes the temperature of the user's location (https://www.timeanddate.com/weather/),
and calculates how many calories the user needs.

Objects: Calorie:
            weight
            height
            age
            sex
            temperature
            calculate()

         Temperature:
            country 
            city
            get()
